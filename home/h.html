Vrindavan
Vrindavan is considered to be a sacred place for Vaisnavism tradition of Hinduism.It is believed that Krishna spent part of his childhood in this city.Along with Vrindavan, all these places are considered to be the center of Radha and Krishna worship.

Golden Temple
The Golden Temple is also known as Harmandir Sahib or the Darbar Sahib.It is one of the holiest sites in Sikhism, alongside the Gurdwara Darbar Sahib Kartarpur in Kartarpur, and Gurdwara Janam Asthan in Nankana Sahib.The Golden Temple is spiritually the most significant shrine in Sikhism

Kedarnath
Kedarnath Temple is a Hindu temple, one of the twelve jyotirlinga of Shiva.The temple is located on the Garhwal Himalayan range near the Mandakini river, in the state of Uttarakhand, India. Due to extreme weather conditions, the temple is open to the general public only between the months of April and November.

Ayodhya
Ayodhya is a city situated on the banks of the Sarayu river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is the administrative headquarters of the Ayodhya district as well as the Ayodhya division of Uttar Pradesh, India.The legendary Ayodhya city is the same as the historical city of Saketa and the present-day Ayodhya
